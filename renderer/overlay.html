Connect to Zoom/FaceTime

To let others hear the AI voice in calls, you need a Virtual Audio Cable.

  1. Install BlackHole 2ch or VB-Cable (Free).
  2. In Relay, select the Cable as Audio Output.
  3. In Zoom/Teams, select the Cable as your Microphone.
